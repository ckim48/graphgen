About the app

Built for clarity and speed

{{ APP_NAME }} focuses on the essentials: safe parsing, crisp plots, and a friction-free UI so you can explore math without distractions.

  • Sympy + NumPy under the hood for accurate sampling
  • Chart.js rendering with grid, axes, and discontinuity handling
  • Works great on laptops, tablets, and classroom projectors
Why it’s different

Explore with smart suggestions

After each plot, the app proposes three follow-ups (e.g., frequency, shift, or inverse) so learners can compare shapes quickly.

  • One-click presets for quick demos
  • Speech input support for equation and ranges
  • Minimal chrome—your graph takes center stage

How TactiGraph Comes Together

From safe parsing to tactile output—step by step.

Step 01

Parse Safely

Sympy-powered parsing with guardrails for clean, predictable input.

Step 02

Plot Clearly

Smart sampling + Chart.js rendering with axes, grid, and gaps at discontinuities.

Step 03

Explore with Hints

One-click suggestions (shift, scale, inverse) to compare shapes fast.

Step 04

Tactile Output

Our First Client

Another way of Seeing (ArtBlind) is an ART_Lab where blind people and artists explore “What does it mean to see?” through arts and technology.

Another way of Seeing (ArtBlind)

Since 1996, ArtBlind has treated blindness as an alternative creative potential and built programs that include arts education, exhibitions, tactile/braille books, and educational apps.

By reframing art as a language for self-expression, ArtBlind supports emotional growth and autonomy for blind youth—expanding access to creative learning and culture.
